## 🤖 AI Integration  

### 🔹 1. Multi-Factor Risk Engine  

The system calculates a **risk score** based on multiple inputs:  

Inputs:  
• Rain probability (%)  
• AQI levels  
• Area risk classification  

Output:  
• Risk Score (0–100)  
• Weekly Premium  

---

### 🔹 2. Income Loss Predictor (Unique Feature)  

WorkSure AI predicts potential income loss before disruption occurs:  

$$ Loss = Income \times RiskFactor $$  

Example:  
• Weekly Income = ₹4000  
• Risk Factor = 0.3  

👉 Predicted Loss = ₹1200  

---

### 🔹 3. Dynamic Premium Calculation  

| Risk Level | Weekly Premium |
|-----------|--------------|
| Low       | ₹10          |
| Medium    | ₹20          |
| High      | ₹30          |

Premiums adjust dynamically based on risk score.

### 🔹 4. AI Fraud Detection Engine  

To prevent misuse, the system performs:

• Claim Pattern Monitoring  

Output:  
✔ Fraud Check: Passed  
⚠ Fraud Check: Flagged

## 🌧 Parametric Triggers  

Claims are automatically triggered when thresholds are crossed:  

• Rainfall > 60mm  
• AQI > 350  
• Flood alerts  
• Local lockdowns  
• Delivery disruptions  

👉 No manual claim process required

## 🔍 Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing Strategy  

To handle GPS spoofing and fraud attacks:  

### 1. Differentiation  

The system distinguishes genuine users from fraudsters using:  

• Real movement patterns  
• Delivery activity logs  
• Weather-location consistency  

---

### 2. Multi-Source Data Analysis  

• Historical movement data  
• Route consistency  
• Time spent in delivery zones  
• Weather verification  

---

### 3. Fraud Ring Detection  

Detects coordinated fraud using:  

• Identical location claims  
• Simultaneous claim spikes  
• Repeated suspicious patterns  

---

### 4. Fair User Experience  

• Suspicious claims are flagged, not rejected  
• Secondary verification is applied  
• Genuine users are protected
